I tend to think problems a great design team can’t solve are few and far between. I'm inspired by the beautiful, imperfect world we live in and the problems that populate it.
I’ve spent my design career listening to users and learning their greatest frustrations - to design digital interfaces they love.
When I’m not in front of my computer, you can find me watching movies, listening to music with the volume turned up too loud, diagramming the perfect sandwich, and workshopping jokes.
